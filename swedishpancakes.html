Swedish Pancakes

Home

Ingredients

2 cups whole milk
1 cup flour
2 eggs

Preparation

  1. Combine and mix milk, flour, and eggs in a bowl

Cooking/Baking

  • Pre-heat skillet/griddle to a medium temperature
  • Melt butter on skillet/griddle
  • Pour 1/3 cup of batter evenly on the skillet/griddle
  • Cook until bottom is light brown (typically 1 to 2 minutes)
  • Flip and continue cooking until second side is light brown
  • Cover with butter and suger before rolling
  • Sprinkle with additional sugar (or any other topings)
