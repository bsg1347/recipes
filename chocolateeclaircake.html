Chocolate Eclair Cake

Home

Cake

Ingredients

1 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
4 eggs

Preparation

  1. Bring water and butter to a boil.
  2. Add flour, stir until it forms to a ball, then remove from heat.
  3. Beat in eggs 1 at a time.
  4. Spread into an ungreased 9 x 13 pan.
  5. Bake at 400 for 30 min.
  6. Press the cake down and let it cool.

Topping

Ingredients

1 large package instant vanilla pudding
1 8oz package cream cheese (softened)

Preparation

  1. Prepare vanilla pudding according to package instructions.
  2. Add cream cheese.
  3. Spread pudding over cake/crust.
  4. Refrigerate 15 - 20 minutes or until set.
  5. Spread with one container of cool whip and drizzle chocolate or butterscotch ice cream topping.
