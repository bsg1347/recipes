Meatballs

Ingredients

1 lb ground hamburger
1 small can evaporated milk
1 egg
1 cup of oatmeal
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tsp salt

Sauce

Ingredients

1/2 cup ketchup
1 tbs mustard
1 cup brown sugar

Preparation

  1. Mix and pour over meatballs.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
