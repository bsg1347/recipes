Classic Macaroni and Cheese

Home

Ingredients

1/2 package (8 oz) Creamette elbow macaroni
3 tbs butter
3 tbs all-purpose flour
2 1/2 cups milk
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 cup minced onion
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Preparation

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 350.
  3. In medium saucepan, melt butter.
  4. Stir in flour.
  5. Gradually stir in milk.
  6. Cook, stirring constantly over medium heat, until sauce thickens.
  7. Stir in salt and pepper.
  8. Add onion and cheese; stir until cheese melts.
  9. Stir cheese sauce into cooked pasta.
  10. Spoon into greased 2-quart baking dish.
  11. Top with bread crumbs.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake 30 minutes.
