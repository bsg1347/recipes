Cheesy Potatoes

Home

Ingredients

1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cups shredded cheese
2 cups sour cream
1/2 cup melted margarine
2 lb hashbrowns - thawed
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Preparation

  1. Place in a 9 x 13 pan.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
  • Add 2 cups of corn flakes to the top with 5 minutes left.
