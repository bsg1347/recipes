Preparation

  1. Heat a small non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Spray pan with vegetable oil and place one corn totilla in pan.
  3. Add 1/4 of the chicken and 1/4 of the cheese, then press a second tortilla on top.
  4. Lightly spray the top tortilla with vegetable oil.
  5. Cook 2 - 3 minutes, or until bottom tortilla begins to brown and cheese begins to melt.
  6. Carefully flip the quesadilla and cook the other side 2 - 3 minutes, or until cheese has melted completely and the other side has browned.
  7. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  8. Cut into wedges and serve warm.
