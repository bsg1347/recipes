Chicken and Cheese Quesadillas

Home

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter
1 package dry Italian salad dressing mix
1 can Campbell's golden mushroom soup
1/2 cup white cooking whine
4 oz onion and chive cream cheese
1 package of angel hair pasta, cooked according to box instructions

Cooking/Baking

  • Place chicken in Crock Pot.
  • In sauce pan, melt butter, stir in dressing mix, soup, cream cheese, and wine.
  • Pour mixture over chicken.
  • Cook on low for 4 - 5 hours.
  • Pour over cooked angel hair pasta.
