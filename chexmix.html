Chex Mix

Home

Ingredients

14 cups Crispix cereal
Cheetos - Lots of them
1 cup nuts
1 cup pretzels
1 stick of butter
1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp onion salt
1 1/2 tsp garlic salt

Preparation

  1. Melt butter, add spices, and mix well.
  2. Drizzle butter/spice mixture over dry ingredients.
  3. Continue stirring butter/spice mixture as you drizzle so the ingredients don't separate.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 250 for 45 minutes, stirring gently every 15 minutes.
  • Pour onto paper towels to cool.
