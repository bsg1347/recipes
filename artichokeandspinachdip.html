Artichoke and Spinach Dip

Home

Ingredients

1 jar (6 oz) marinated artichoke hearts
1 package (9 to 11 oz) frozen creamed spinach, thawed
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1 small garlic clove, pressed
1/2 cup grated fresh parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Coarsely chop artichokes.
  2. Add spinach, mayo, and sour cream.
  3. Add garlic and parmesan cheese.
  4. Mix together.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 375 for 20 - 25 minutes or until heated through.
