Snicker Doodle

Home

Ingredients

1 cup butter softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 3/4 cups flour
2 tsp cream of tarter
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Mix together flour, cream of tarter, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  1. Cream together sugar and butter. Add eggs and vanilla and blend well.
  1. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix well.
  1. Shape dough into 1 inch balls and roll in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  1. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake for 8-10 minutes at 350 degrees.
