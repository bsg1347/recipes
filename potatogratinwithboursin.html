Potato Gratin with Boursin

Home

Ingredients

2 cups heavy cream
1 package (5 oz) Boursin cheese with herbs
3 pounds small red potatoes, unpeeled and thinly sliced
Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
1 1/2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Butter a 13 x 9 inch casserole dish.
  3. In a food processor, combine cream and Boursin cheese until smooth.
  4. Arrange half of the potatoes in backing dish in slightly overlapping rows.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Spoon one half cheese mixture over potatoes.
  7. Arrange remaining potatoes in dish and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Spoon remaining cheese mixture over casserole.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake until potatoes are golden brown (1 hour).
  • Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
