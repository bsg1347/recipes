Penne Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

1 lb hamburger
1 jar spaghetti sauce
3 cups penne noodles
1 package shredded parmesan cheese
1 package mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  1. Brown hamburger, mix in sauce and 1/2 package of parmesan cheese.
  2. Cook noodles and combine with hamburger mix.
  3. Place in backing dish and top with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Serve with chips.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
