Star of Bethlehem Cookies

Home

Ingredients

1 3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup shortening (butter flavor Crisco)
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 egg
2 T milk
1 tsp vanilla

Preparation

  1. Mix sugars, shortening, and peanut butter together.
  1. Mix in egg, milk, and vanilla.
  1. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  1. Roll into balls and then roll in sugar.

Cooking/Baking

  • Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
  • Place a star in the middle when removed from the oven.
