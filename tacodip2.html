Taco Dip #2

Home

Ingredients

16 ounces of sour cream
16 ounces of cream cheese
1 pkg of taco seasoning
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup of diced tomatoes
1/2 cup of chopped green onion
1 large bag of Mexican blend grated cheese

Preparation

  1. Blend sour cream, cream cheese, and taco seasoning.
  2. Spread evenly on serving tray or plate.
  3. Layer remaining ingredients in any order, saving the cheese for last to spread evenly.
  4. Serve with chips.
