Gourmet Beef Tenderloin

Home

Ingredients

1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 cup chopped green onions
4 tbs butter, melted
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 fully trimmed beef tenderloin (4 - 5 pounds)
1/2 tsp seasoned salt
1/4 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
8 ounces red wine vinegar and oil dressing
Crushed black peppercorns
Whole fresh mushrooms for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a skillet, saute sliced mushrooms and green onions in butter until tender.
  2. Drain, stir in parsley and set aside.
  3. Cut tenderloin lengthwise through middle to 1/4 inch from each end.
  4. Sprinkle outside with seasoned salt lemon pepper seasoning.
  5. Spoon mushroom mixture into center opening and sprinkle with blue cheese.
  6. Close tenderloin, tying with heavy string at 2 inch intervals to secure and then place in a large backing pan.
  7. Pour dressing over tenderloin.
  8. Cover and regrigerate 8 hours, basting occasionally.

Cooking/Baking

  • Preheat over to 350.
  • Remove tenderloin to a baking pan and discard marinade.
  • Top with peppercorns and bake 45 - 55 minutes or until meat thermometer registers 120 or desired degree of doneness.
  • Transfer to serving platter and let stand 10 - 15 minutes before carving.
  • Remove strings, slice, and server garnished with mushrooms.
