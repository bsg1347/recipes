Macaroni Salad

Home

Ingredients

1/2 package of ring macaroni
Miracle Whip
Frozen peas
Pickles
Velveeta cheese
Cucumbers
Tomatoes

Preparation

  1. Cook and drain macaroni.
  2. Cut Velveeta cheese, pickles, cucumbers, and tomatoes into small cubes.
  3. Add Miracle Whip to ingredients until desired thickness is reached.
