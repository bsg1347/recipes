Cheerio Bars

Home

Ingredients

6 1/2 cups of Cheerios
1 stick of butter
1 cup of peanut butter
1 cup of sugar
1 cup of Karol syrup (white/clear)
Chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Melt butter in a pan.
  2. Once melted, add 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of Karol syrup and stir together.
  3. Turn up heat until it boils.
  4. Once bubbles appear, turn off heat and add 1 cup peanut butter.
  5. Stir until melted and pour over the Cheerios in a large bowl.
  6. Stir until the Cheerios are covered, then pour one layer into a 9 x 12 pan.
  7. Add chocolate chips.
  8. Pour another layer and add additional chocolate chips.
  9. Let cool for at least 4 - 5 hours.

Additional Notes

  • If bars are too hard after cooling, the butter and sugar mix boiled too long.
