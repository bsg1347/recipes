Honey-Poppyseed Cornish Hens

Home

Ingredients

2 rock Cornish hens
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/3 cup honey
1 tbs poppyseed
1 1/2 tsp ground mustard
3/4 tsp ground ginger

Preparation

  1. Heat over to 350.
  2. Spray roast pan.
  3. Sprinkle hens with salt and pepper.
  4. Mix remaining ingrediants and brush over hens.

Cooking/Baking

  • Roast uncovered until done.
